About Me






Hi there! My name is Caroline, and I am completing my Msc Software Design & Development at NUIG. Welcome to my passion project! When I'm not coding I love to travel and visit friends.











I created this website out of the desire to have a place for unbiased news. Here, you can read a variety of different perspectives and views. This website is designed to push you out of your comfort zone and present you news you may not otherwise see. It is my hope that this website leads us to become more informed voters.
